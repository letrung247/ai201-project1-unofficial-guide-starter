What are the meal plans for 2025–26 at Augustana?

Meal plans at Augustana are prepaid dining accounts used for on-campus food purchases. They are set up at the start of each academic term and charged to a student’s tuition bill. Each plan 
includes a set number of meal swipes plus Viking Bucks, which are loaded onto a student ID card and automatically deducted when used.

First-year and sophomore students are automatically enrolled in the Any 15 plan each semester. This plan provides 15 meals per week and includes Viking Bucks. Students can change their plan 
during the first two weeks of the semester through the housing and dining portal or by contacting Dining Services.

Junior and senior students are not automatically assigned a meal plan but can purchase any available option at any time.

Meal plans can be used across campus dining locations, including the Gerber Dining Center, Brew by the Slough, Westerlin Market, and Gus’s Snack Bar. However, the Gerber Unlimited plan is 
restricted to meal swipes used only in the Gerber Dining Center.

There are multiple plan types depending on how often students eat on campus. The Gerber Unlimited plan provides unlimited access to the dining hall plus Viking Bucks and retail meal swipes for
other locations. Other plans such as Any 19, Any 15, Any 12, and Any 10 provide a fixed number of meals per week along with varying amounts of Viking Bucks. Some off-campus plans like Any 80 
and Any 100 provide a fixed number of meals per term rather than per week.

Meal swipes can be used for dine-in meals or certain to-go options, and most plans allow up to four swipes per day. Weekly meal plans reset each Sunday and do not roll over unused meals. 
Semester-based plans and Viking Bucks may roll over depending on the plan, but all unused balances expire at the end of the academic year.

Viking Bucks function like campus cash and can be used in all dining locations and concessions. They can also be reloaded at a dollar-for-dollar rate, with a 10% bonus when purchasing $500 or 
more at once. Unused Viking Bucks generally roll over during the academic year but expire after commencement.
