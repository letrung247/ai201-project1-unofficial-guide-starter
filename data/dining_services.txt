What is dining like at Augustana?

The dining system at Augustana includes several on-campus locations designed to provide both full meals and quick food options throughout the day. Main dining areas include the Gerber Dining 
Center, Brew by the Slough, Westerlin Market, and Gus’s Snack Bar, each serving different schedules and food styles.

Regular dining hours vary by location. The Gerber Dining Center is open 7 a.m.–8 p.m. Monday through Thursday, 7 a.m.–7 p.m. on Friday, 9 a.m.–7 p.m. on Saturday, and 9 a.m.–8 p.m. on Sunday.
Brew by the Slough operates 7:30 a.m.–8 p.m. Monday through Thursday, 7:30 a.m.–5 p.m. on Friday, and 11 a.m.–3 p.m. on weekends. Westerlin Market is open late, from 2 p.m.–midnight Monday 
through Thursday and Sunday, and 2–8 p.m. on Friday and Saturday. Gus’s Snack Bar is open weekdays from 9:30 a.m.–2:30 p.m.

The Gerber Dining Center is the main dining hall located in the Gerber Center for Student Life. It features multiple made-to-order stations offering cuisines such as Chinese, Indian, Italian,
and Mexican food, along with salads, soups, seafood, burgers, sandwiches, and desserts. The menu rotates every four weeks and changes daily. It also accommodates dietary needs including 
vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and Halal options.

Dining services emphasize sustainability by sourcing produce and meat from on-campus Augie Acres and local farms, using biodegradable containers, and partnering with local growers to compost 
food waste.

Brew by the Slough offers coffee drinks, pastries, and grab-and-go food in a café-style space with an outdoor patio. It also hosts student performances and local entertainment.

Westerlin Market, located in the residence hall, provides snacks and convenience items throughout the day and night, making it a late-access option for students.

Gus’s Snack Bar offers lighter meals such as sandwiches and quick bites for students in or near the Brunner Theatre Center.

Overall, Augustana dining is structured to give students flexible access to meals, from full dining hall experiences to quick snacks and late-night options across campus.
