Dining Locations and Hours

Gerber Center Dining Room

The Gerber Center Dining Room offers casual dining at six food stations with different cuisines, multiple beverage stations and the Breakfast Bar. It is located
on the fifth floor of the Gerber Center for Student Life.

This is an all-you-care-to-eat facility. Breakfast is $10; lunch is $11.50; and dinner is $11.50. Carryout meals are the same price.

The marketplace style dining stations are:

American Grill, featuring grilled sandwiches, quesadillas, fries, Viking Subs and Burgers.

The Carvery, with classic favorites and chef specialties.

Wild Thymes, offering vegetarian and vegan options.

Green Creations, with sandwiches, wraps, soups, a salad bar, and fruit and yogurt bar.

Global Fusion, specializing in international foods.

Bella Luna, offering Italian cuisine including fresh-baked pizzas and made-to-order pasta dishes.

Söt, featuring pastries and desserts from the campus bakery.

The Breakfast Bar is open all day and includes cereals, bagels, muffins, waffles, and more.

Beverage stations offer juices, milk, sodas, coffee, and tea.

Dining Services hosts special events including Fresh Fruit Friday, Midnight Breakfast, and holiday menus throughout the year.

Meal Bundles

Available at all locations except the Gerber Dining Center.

Includes one entrée, two sides or chips, and a drink.

Entrées include sandwiches, salads, wraps, parfaits, and veggie or fruit trays. Sides include pasta salad, cottage cheese, carrots, apple slices, fruit, cheese,
or chips. Drinks may be canned or bottled.
