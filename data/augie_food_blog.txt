What's good to eat at Augie?

The dining hall in the Gerber Center is one of four options on campus where you can get food. The other areas include Gus's Snack Bar in the Brunner Theatre Center,
 the Westerlin Market in the Westerlin Dorm, and the Brew by the Slough in the Gerber Center.

All of those options allow you to use your meal swipes or pay with cash. If you're unsure of what meal plan to get, check out the dining hall website. 
You'll find information about meal plans, hours, calendars, and catering.

You have endless possibilities and unique options for meals throughout the week. My favorites are Mondays, Fridays, and Sundays in the dining hall and Wednesdays
 at Gus's Snack Bar.

On Monday nights, it's breakfast for dinner. Students can enjoy sausage links, scrambled eggs, and hashbrowns.

Fridays are Fresh Fruit Fridays. The dining hall offers strawberries, blueberries, bananas, apples, and other fruits with lunch. It is popular with students 
and staff.

On Sundays, the Gerber dining hall offers a build-your-own sundae bar with ice cream and toppings.

The dining hall also serves a variety of meals throughout the week, including a mac and cheese bar.

The mac and cheese bar is described as one of the most popular dining options.

On Wednesdays, Gus's Snack Bar serves Walking Tacos. Students can choose Fritos or Doritos as a base and add toppings such as beans, taco-seasoned beef, lettuce, 
tomatoes, olives, and salsa.

The dining areas provide both food and a social atmosphere where students can eat with friends or on their own.
