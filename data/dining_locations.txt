Dining options at Augie

The Gerber Center Dining Room is the main dining hall on campus, located on the fifth floor of the Gerber Center for Student Life. It is 
all-you-care-to-eat and includes stations like American Grill, Carvery, Global Fusion, Bella Luna, Green Creations, Wild Thymes, Söt bakery,
and an all-day Breakfast Bar. Meals are served daily with rotating hours: weekday breakfast, lunch, and dinner, plus brunch on weekends.

Gävle Grab&Go dining room offers quick meals and seating, serving lunch and dinner on weekdays and lunch on Friday.

Westerlin Market is a convenience store in Westerlin Residence Hall with snacks, drinks, frozen meals, and essentials. It is open daily 
with extended evening hours on weekdays.

Brew by the Slough is a café in the Gerber Center offering coffee drinks, pastries, soups, and grab-and-go food. It is open mornings through
evening on weekdays and shorter weekend hours.

Gus’ Snack Bar is in the Brunner Theater Center and serves snacks plus made-to-order items like burgers and walking tacos, open weekdays 
during lunch hours.

Meal bundles are available at most locations and include an entrée, two sides or chips, and a drink.
